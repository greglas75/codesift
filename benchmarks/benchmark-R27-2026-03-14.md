## Key Insight: top_k=5 vs top_k=3

With only ~600 token difference (51,081 vs 50,483), increasing top_k from 3 to 5/7 adds ~1 quality point (7.5→8.5). The quality-per-token efficiency strongly favors top_k=5/7.

| Parameter | Tokens | Quality | Token/Quality ratio |
|-----------|--------|---------|---------------------|
| top_k=3 (R26) | ~50,483 | 7.5/10 | 6,731 tokens/point |
| top_k=5/7 (R27) | ~51,081 | 8.5/10 | 6,010 tokens/point |

**R27 is the better balance.**
