## Critical Finding: Form Answers Save ~18K Tokens

Comparing R26 (form, top_k=3) vs R18 (prose, top_k=5):
- R26: **~50,483 tokens**
- R18: **69,179 tokens**
- Difference: **~18,696 tokens (~27% reduction)**

The form-style answer format eliminates:
- Planning verbosity and preamble
- Explanatory prose in answers
- Closing summaries
Total savings ~18K regardless of query structure.

This is the **dominant factor** in token optimization — far larger than any retrieval parameter change.
